# **App Name**: FitFemAI

## Core Features:

- Personalized Workout Plan: Generates a weekly workout plan tailored to the user's fitness level, goals (weight loss, tone, maintain, muscle gain), available equipment (weights/no weights), and dietary preferences.
- Exercise Customization: Allows users to modify the duration of each exercise within a routine and automatically adjusts calorie burn calculations accordingly.
- Weight Mode Adaptation: Automatically suggests weight or no-weight exercise variations based on user input, adjusting calorie burn calculation based on the weight entered.
- AI Calorie Burn Tracking Tool: Calculates calorie burn per exercise and total calories burned per day based on MET, weight, time, and optional weight input. Considers when or if to incorporate new input based on reasonable calculation bounds.
- Food Logging System: Allows users to log their daily food intake, search from a database including common Indian and Western foods, add custom foods, and track calories, protein, carbs, fats, sugar, and fiber.
- AI Calorie Budget Recommendation Tool: Recommends a daily calorie intake based on the user's goals (weight loss, maintain, gain) and tracks remaining calories, considering intake, burned, and goal deficit. Includes food database.
- Barcode Scanning: Enables users to scan packaged food barcodes to automatically fill nutrition values if the device supports a camera.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Vibrant purple (#A076F9) to convey energy and sophistication.
- Background color: Light purple (#E6E0F8), a very desaturated shade of the primary color, providing a calm backdrop.
- Accent color: Pink (#F472B6), an analogous color that provides a noticeable contrast.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif providing a modern look.
- Use clean, modern icons representing exercises, food categories, and metrics.
- Emphasize a clean, spacious layout, promoting ease of navigation and readability on various screen sizes.
- Incorporate subtle animations and transitions to guide users through the workout and food logging process.
